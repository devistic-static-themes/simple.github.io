Welcome to Cafe Italiano. The Cafe Italiano is Pakistan's most recognized and distinguished hospitality brand with a history of market innovation and excellence in restaurants chain operation. The Cafe Italiano Restaurants are regarded and respected as the number one restaurant entity in Pakistan by local and foreign observers.

Our Value

A combination of the cultural and the culinary, Cafe Italiano specializes in resurrecting the cuisine of the Italian, offering patrons a ‘mock Italian’ dining experience which evokes the palatial luxury of a traditional table. The themed restaurant experience provides outdoor dining in castle-like surroundings which bring out ‘the legendary grandeur of a splendid and noble past’. The cuisine meanwhile is a combination of Italian, French, Barbecue and Traditional Pakistani, all of which can be enjoyed from Cafe Italiano’s extensive buffet service. The restaurant regularly introduces new dishes and holds special events and performances.

History

The Cafe Italiano Restaurants at Mall and Liberty Market Karachi, are considered to be the first proper family style restaurants in the food industry of Pakistan. These restaurants pride themselves for being symbolized as an eatery with a customer loyalty spanning over three generations. As a restaurant model, these have inspired the set-up of numerous other restaurants across Pakistan..
